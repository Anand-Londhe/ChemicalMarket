Graphite Carbon Nitride(g-C3N4) is a polymeric material composed of tris-triazine-based patterns with a C/N ratio of 3/4 and a small amount of H. Due to its stacked structure, g-C3N4 is often considered as sp2-hybridized nitrogen-substituted graphene.
