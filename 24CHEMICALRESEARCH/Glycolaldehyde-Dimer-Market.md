Definition

Glycolaldehyde dimer is a chemical compound formed by the dimerization of two molecules of glycolaldehyde. It is commonly used in various industrial applications, including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and research. Glycolaldehyde itself is an important intermediate in prebiotic chemistry and is significant in the synthesis of various organic compounds.
