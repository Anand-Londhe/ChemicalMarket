Flexible firestop sealants are specialized sealants that prevent the spread of fire by sealing openings in building constructions, floors, ceilings, or wall assemblies. They have fire-resistant properties, reduce smoke and toxins, act as acoustic barriers, and are paintable and easy to clean with water.
